Expediting TTS Synthesis with Adversarial Vocoding

*Paarth Neekhara, *Chris Donahue, Miller Puckette, Shlomo Dubnov, Julian McAuley

We present sound examples for our experiments in "Expediting TTS Synthesis with Adversarial Vocoding". Each mel-spectrogram is vocoded using different methods and we present the vocoded audio for 5 such randomly selected mel-spectrogram representations from the LJ Speech Dataset. Additionally, we present TTS examples for vocoding Tacotron-2 generated mel-spectrograms for some jokes.

Ablation study: magnitude and phase estimation

Sound examples for ablating the heuristics for magnitude and phase estimation for vocoding mel spectrograms. It can be observed that coupling an ideal estimation of phase or magnitude with a heuristic for the other yields audio quality that is marginally worse than the original audio.

Real Mel Spectrograms

Sound examples for vocoding mel-spectrograms of audio utterances from our test data set.

Tacotron-2 Generated Mel Spectrograms

Sound examples for vocoding Tacotron-2 generated synthetic mel-spectrograms for audio transcriptions of the same set of audio utterances as used in the other examples.

Jokes

Sound examples for vocoding Tacotron-2 generated synthetic mel-spectrograms for some jokes :)
